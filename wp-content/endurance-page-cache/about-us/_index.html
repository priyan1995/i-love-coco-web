I Love Coco

Ethical Business Policy Statement

The story behind the brand name I Love Coco and the healthy, organic and fresh coconut products we produce stems from the well-reputed name Jaindi exports (Pvt) Ltd.

The company is primarily based in Sri Lanka as in known and recognized around the island. However, it has been taking international wholesale markets by storm due to the delectable, diverse and immaculate product range that has caught the eyes of many renowned international and wholesale buyers from near and far.

The tiny island paradise of Sri Lanka is known for its coastlines fringed with coconut trees and thus coconut is an expanding industry that Jaindi exports (Pvt) Ltd has advanced in many ways. Furthermore, the company further offers a diversified product portfolio from organic spices to Ayurvedic beverages in constant supply to the global market.

Vision

To become the preferred supplier and the largest organic products exporter in the world with the reputation for quality, safety and the capacity.

Mission

Produce and provide high quality organic coconut products that are manufactured protecting the nutritional compositions with zero addictives

“From humble beginnings to where we are today- the journey has been a long one but through it all we have grown, expanded and have worked on improving our capacity and resources. We have been regarded and acknowledged over the years as a trusted supplier for international brands and earned a good reputation in the international markets.

It does not stop there, success is not limited to the financial realm as over the years we have heavily invested in CSR projects that enable us to help farmers living below the poverty line.

As a company we are grateful for the support and look forward to introduce more organic products and make a significant contribution to the socio-economic development of Sri Lanka.


Sampath Anuruddha

Founder & Managing Director – I love Coco

How We Are Different

Exemplary Products- Set Above the Rest

Locally Sourced

At I Love Coco, the coconuts are all locally and organically grown in our own land. The coconuts are harvested and collected by our trusted local farmers and are graded and controlled of quality accordance with a set standard before they arrive at the processing plants for the processing procedure.

Coconut Processing

Each product requires a different processing process that will in turn ensure its quality and pave the way towards organic and fresh products. Primarily, the husk is removed to reduce the micro-organisms that are contaminated on the surface. To prevent further browning the coconuts are dipped in a solution.

Coconut Manufacturing

A step later from processing is perhaps the most endearing part of the production process. This step has coconuts crushed, filtered or dry under stringent rules and regulations and on the overseeing of trained professionals that go out of their way to check in on the quality and safety of the process at hand.

Quality Check

Every single after-product is subjected to a quality check by professionals in the field. It is our guarantee that each nut is checked thoroughly to ensure high quality products goes into the market. Agriculture chemicals, heavy metals and bacteria are checked in a modern laboratory with the latest technology.
